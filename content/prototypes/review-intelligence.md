## How it works

Paste a venue's Google Maps URL, and the tool pulls real reviews from Google and TripAdvisor via Outscraper API. Claude AI scores every review on Food, Service, and Atmosphere (1-5). For wedding venues, it also scrapes Lemon8 and Bridely and adds a 4th dimension: Coordination.

The AI then discovers 10 competitors (5 direct, 5 indirect), scrapes and scores their reviews, and produces a full ranking table with gap analysis. Editable strategic recommendations are generated referencing actual competitor names and score gaps.

## Who it's for

Marketing managers, venue GMs, and the 1-HOST weddings team. Each person runs their assigned venue(s) weekly — the tool handles the data extraction and scoring so the team focuses on action.

## Key features

- **4 review sources**: Google Reviews, TripAdvisor, Lemon8, Bridely
- **AI scoring**: Food / Service / Atmosphere / Coordination (weddings)
- **Competitor benchmarking**: 10 competitors discovered, scored, and ranked
- **Weekly trends**: Save weekly snapshots, track month-on-month movement
- **Competitor lock**: Same 10 competitors benchmarked every week for consistency
- **Wedding mode**: Strict keyword filter — only genuine wedding reviews pass through
- **Team recognition**: Named staff extracted from reviews with sentiment tracking
- **Editable strategy**: AI recommendations you can edit, delete, expand with "Add More"
- **Word exports**: Every tab produces an editable .docx report

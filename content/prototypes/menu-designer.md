## How it works

Chef inputs the season, cuisine direction, target price point, and any dietary constraints. The assistant generates 3-5 menu concepts with dish ideas, flavour profiles, and rough cost estimates. Chef picks a direction, iterates, then exports a draft PDF for F&B review.

## Who it's for

Head chefs and sous chefs planning seasonal refreshes or one-off tasting menus.

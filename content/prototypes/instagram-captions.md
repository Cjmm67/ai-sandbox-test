## What we tried

Generated 3-5 caption options per post from a brief (photo, venue, occasion). Integrated with the scheduling tool via Zapier.

## Why it was killed

- Captions lacked the venue-specific voice our brand relies on
- Social team spent more time editing AI output than writing from scratch
- Audience engagement dropped 18% during the trial period

## What we learned

- Venue voice is not a template problem — it's a judgement problem
- AI captions work when there's no brand voice to protect (e.g. customer service replies); they don't work when distinctive voice IS the value
- A better future attempt would be a "voice brief" generator that helps the social team think, not a caption generator that replaces them
